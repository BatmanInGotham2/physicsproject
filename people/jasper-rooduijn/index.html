My Story?

Jasper is one of the partners and the technical chief of the Rodesk Tribe. As a professor of development, Jasper knows everything - literally everything - about the parts of digital products most people never get to see. He spends most of his time working 'under the hood' of the products we create. With his solid vision on technical processes his goal is to inspire the Rodesk developers and determine the long term technical strategy.

Happy People

I love technology. My main goal is using the power of technology to empower people, making their lives easier and more enjoyable. If I manage to make people smile along the way, that makes me a happier person myself.
