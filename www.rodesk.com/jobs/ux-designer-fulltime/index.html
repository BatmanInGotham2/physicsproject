User Experience Design Agency

Fulltime UX Designer

11 May 2016 / Rodesk

Do you feel like joining a group of young creative online fanatics in developing ambitious and innovative products?

Our goal: creating unique online experiences serving to turn users and moderators into happy people.

As a Rodesk UX designer, we provide you with the time and space you need to develop yourself and to further expand and deepen your areas of expertise and interest. Get stuck in and join other designers, front end developers and UX experts in challenging projects. Of course, we balance our hard work with plenty of play, so we make sure there’s always time for a fierce battle of ping pong or pool between jobs.

If you feel up to the challenge and think you could teach us a trick or two, then don’t hesitate to contact us and let us know why we can’t do without you!

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Collaborating with senior designers, writers, developers and strategists on a wide range of interesting digital projects;
  • Designing responsive websites and other creative digital user experiences to an elite standard, meeting clients’ strategic business objectives;
  • Creating user-flows and prototypes;
  • Providing design and concept direction to junior designers and freelancers;
  • Ensuring design quality and consistency throughout project life cycles, with rigorous testing across multiple platforms and devices;
  • Assisting in the evolution of studio practices.

What We Can Offer

  • A sweet spot to work in our Rotterdam Creative Factory studio;
  • An enthusiastic and youthful team of web specialists;
  • A wealth of expertise and experience;
  • Personal guidance including growth plans and career opportunities;
  • Challenging tasks in customer projects and side projects;
  • Plenty of responsibility, freedom, and time for personal interests;

Requirements/Must Haves

  • Online portfolio showcasing exemplary design skills and current experience
  • Proficiency in Photoshop, Illustrator and Sketch
  • Ability to produce optimized assets for builds
  • Experience in prototyping and animation
  • Understanding of video editing is a plus
  • Team oriented, but self motivated
  • A passion for utting edge technology and trends within our industry
