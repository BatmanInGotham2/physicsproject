Background

Although BLOC occupies a rebel position within their market of operations, their rebel nature was insufficiently manifest in their existing online presence. As a youthful organisation, they yearned to take the next step forward and boost the impact of their professional profile.

Our Mission

We aimed for a visual translation of BLOC's radical nature in a website that had to be genuinely different. We made good use of the creative liberty BLOC granted us by opting for a cheeky font paired with an excitingly unorthodox content arrangement.

The result is a distinctive website perfectly suited for BLOC's active use by publishing on their projects and spreading all the latest news.

Brand & Identity

Getting the BLOC's distinct character across online called for accentuation of their existing identity. This more pronounced identity was achieved by teaming up with household photographer Kevin Krebbers to furnish a personal imagery bank, which has added immensely to BLOC's marked online presence.

Next Generation Development

BLOC develops pioneering concepts and turn ideas into business. They believe innovative collaboration requires an inspiring work environment. So, they are located in a intimate café in Rotterdam, the architectural capital of the Netherlands. BLOC’s doors are always open for to invite you in for conversation or brainstorm sessions.

Global Operations

The BLOC team is cooperating on every scale, in every corner of the world. Their projects range from Rotterdam and Paris to Dubai. A young organisation with great ambitions. We felt this ambition and we developed this daring website together with BLOC.
